Margin/Padding/Border

This box demonstrates the margin property. I applied the margin to this paragraph tag INSIDE the box. This creates a bigger gap between the border of the outer box and the border of the paragraph. As an added bonus, I also made the border of the paragraph dotted.

This box demonstrates the padding property. I changed the margin of the paragraph to zero. So, now the outer box border is in contact with the paragraph border, but notice that there's more space between the text and the solid border of this paragraph beacuse I increased the padding. Isn't it magical?
